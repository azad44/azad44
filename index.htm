Faculty of Science

The Faculty has highly efficient and well experienced teaching staff members providing the students with high quality courses using modern teaching and practical facilities to fulfill the market requirements with qualified graduates. The duration of the studyin the Faculty of science is four academic years and the graduate will obtain B.Sc. Degree in Science. The Faculty is also offering postgraduate programs (M.Sc. and Ph.D.) in various disciplines. The following is showing faculty's departments:

School of Natural Sciences

  • Department of Biology
  • Department of Chemistry
  • Department of Environmental Sciences

School of Pure Sciences

  • Department of Physics
  • Department of Computer Science
  • Department of Mathematics

For more information visit Faculty website

Faculty of Humanities

The faculty has active teaching staff members dedicated to teaching and research in different disciples in the field of humanities. For undergraduate studies, the faculty offers BA degree in the fields of Kurdish, Arabic, English, Turkish Languages as well as History, and Islamic Studies. Furthermore, the Faculty also awards MA and PhD degrees in various disciples. The following is showing faculty's departments:

School of arts

  • Department of History
  • Department of Islamic Studies

School of language

  • Department of Kurdish Language
  • Department of English Language
  • Department of Arabic Language
  • Department of Turkish Language

For more information visit Faculty website

Faculty of Education

The goal of the faculty, like any other faculty, is to build an educated society to play a vital role in development of education sector. It enables the students to become professional and successful educational leaders. The students will receive their Bachelors Degree in a period of four years. After these four years the students will have opportunities for post-graduate studies in the Kurdistan region or abroad. The following is showing faculty's departments:

  • Department of General Psychology
  • Department of Physical Education
  • Department of Mathematics
  • Department of Geography

For more information visit Faculty website

College of Medicine

The College of Medicine at the University of Zakho was established in September 2018. We are an academic medical institute dedicated to provide outstanding learning education system and training with very early clinical experiences in order to generate highly medical qualified, and caring excellent physicians. Zakho Medical College aims to integrate the learning of basic and clinical sciences from an early stage of training, based on a platform of professionalism. The following is showing college's departments:

  • Department of Medical Education Development
  • Department of Biomedical Science

For more information visit College website

College of Engineering

The college has highly efficient and experienced teaching staff providing students with high quality programs and using modern teaching and practical facilities to fulfil the industry requirements. The following is showing college's departments:

  • Department of Petroleum Engineering
  • Department of Mechanical Engineering

For more information visit College website

College of Basic Education

The College of Basic Education has been opened in the academic year 2017-2018. The goal of opening such college is for improving the education sciences for both students and teachers, and prepare them to become specialists and academics in education, who in turn will improve the educational field in our society. Meanwhile, this college will provide the teachers who already work in the ministry of education with the improved skills in many various disciplines. The duration of studying in this college extends for four academic years and the graduates will receive BSc Degree in Education, or BA Degree in Education in the public and private specialties according to their choices, which qualifies them to do their work in the field of education. The following is showing college's departments:

  • Department of Kurdish Language
  • Department of Arabic Language
  • Department of English Language
  • Department of Social Sciences
  • Department of General Sciences

For more information visit College website

College of Administration and Economics

The college aimed to teach the basic knowledge and practical skills for modern commerce, banking and business economics. The College of Administration and Economics is very important for the modern era. It was opened to increase students’ knowledge in business and provide them with skills that will help them to deal with the complexities of the business world, both nationally and internationally. The following is showing college's departments:

  • Department of Banking & Financial Sciences
  • Department of Economic Sciences
  • Department of Management Sciences

For more information visit College website

Evening Studies

Evening studies of the University of Zakho play an important role related to the teachers of basic education who are following the Ministry of Education. According to the suggested plan of the University of Zakho for the academic year 2017-2018, the number of the accepted students have been increased for the available departments of the evening studies as shown in the following:
